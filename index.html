Research

I am broadly interested in artificial intelligence, and believe that unsupervised multimodal learning is a good step in the direction of more general AI. My research focus in Prof. Canny's lab is building self-supervised learning methods for video perception. This involves learning representations across large-scale vision, audio and natural language data.
